Orne Publishing Designs

01 - Logo for Orne Publishing

This logo references some of my favorite marks from old publishers. I love the use of animals, buildings, ships, etc., mixed with the authentic feel of letter press printing.

02 - Design for the Orne Publishing website

In the design for this site I wanted to play with different elements from journals, notebooks, old page textures, ornaments, etc., and merge them into an interface that felt more like a boutique shop than a large publisher.
