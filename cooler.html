Cooler Museum

02 - Concept Logo for Cooler Museum

Several years back Salem, MA requested proposals for adaptive reuse of the Old Salem Jail site. I helped put together a team of other business owners, investors, and artists from the area and together we submitted a proposal. Our vision was to turn the site into a contemporary art museum, the Cooler Museum. Alas, it became condos. A twenty plus page proposal and a pretty sweet logo are all that remain.
