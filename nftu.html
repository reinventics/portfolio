Notes From The Underground

01 - CD cover (left) and Poster Design

Notes From The Underground was a Boston-based recording series that helped young musicians make their debut recordings. We focused on recruiting top piano students from schools including Juilliard, Berklee, New England Conservatory, Boston Conservatory, etc., and taking them through the process of making an album.

02 - Promotional Video

This was an elevator pitch video that was created and used to quickly give musicians and potential collaborators an idea of what we were up to.

03 - Recording Stage at Steinert Hall

A total of 11 recordings were produced between late 1999 and 2002 in this 100 year-old concert hall located 3 stories underground in Boston (closed to the public since the 1930's). Despite its crumbling interior the hall has somehow preserved its ideal acoustics. We pushed 9 foot Steinway concert grands out onto a small stage, mic'd them up, and made some outstanding recordings.
